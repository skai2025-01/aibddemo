Convenient Offline Payment Choices

For those who prefer traditional methods, you can settle your payments through:

  • Over-the-Counter Deposits: Present your bill statement/reference number at any branch of DBP nationwide.
  • Accredited Payment Centers: Utilize our network of authorized payment partners such as Bayad Center, SM Bills Payment, and others. A complete list is available on the official SBMA website.
  • Bank Transfer: Directly deposit to SBMA's official account at DBP. Account details will be clearly indicated on your bill statement.

Always retain your official payment receipt for seamless reconciliation.
